Who We Are

About IM Engineering

IM Engineering & Technologies, founded in 2018 by Vigneshwar Elangovan, is a Chennai-based precision metrology company offering qualified dimensional inspection services to a wide range of industries.

We provide on-site CMM inspection facility using the Hexagon Absolute Arm 8525 with RS5 3D Scanner — achieving 0.025mm probing accuracy and 0.048mm scanning accuracy over a 2500mm measuring range. Analysis is performed using PolyWorks Inspector Premium (Innovmetric, Version 2019 IR7).

Our portable CMM service motto — "At Anywhere, At Anytime" — reflects our commitment to bringing world-class metrology to your factory floor. We measure on machine clamping condition, at any ground level, in any axis.

We serve Tier-1 automotive suppliers, OEMs, and industrial manufacturers — including Brakes India, Caterpillar, Doowon, Varroc, Uno Minda, TAFE, SL Lumax, SMR/SMRC, Caparo, Kumar Tools, Blue Star, Roop Polymers, Saargummi, and TI India.
